# Setup Guide

Congratulations on your new CDN service!

Right after your purchase, you will receive an email titled "CDN Welcome Email". This email will provide the following details:

> We’re excited to let you know that everything is ready to begin setting up your CDN resource proxy cache.
>
> To start the installation process, please submit a ticket through your client portal. Be sure to include:
>
> * your project or server name,&#x20;
> * the IP, and,
> * the ports used by your server (e.g., FiveM default game port is 30120).
>
> Please note: Tickets submitted without this information will be closed without a response.
>
> Let’s get your files delivered faster and help reduce your players’ ping!

Your next steps will be to create a support ticket through your client portal and kindly await our instructions, thank you!

The setup for your CDN is a cooperative effort between you, the user, and us, the host. It takes both our administrative end and your comprehension to correctly set up your CDN!
