# Donations

**1of1 Servers: Giving Back to the Community**

At 1of1 Servers, we believe in supporting meaningful causes and making a difference in our community and beyond. Each month, we proudly contribute to organizations that align with our mission to create a better world. Below are the initiatives we support:

***

#### **Lionfish Central**

**Website**: [lionfishcentral.org](https://lionfishcentral.org/)

We care deeply about our oceans, especially as a company based in Florida. Lionfish Central is dedicated to cleaning up our oceans, particularly in Boynton Beach, Florida, by addressing the invasive lionfish problem. Their efforts help restore marine ecosystems, and we are honored to support their mission.

***

#### **St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital**

**Website**: [stjude.org](https://www.stjude.org/)

This cause holds a special place in our hearts. A close family member was diagnosed with leukemia at the age of four and survived, thanks to groundbreaking treatments. St. Jude leads the way in treating and defeating childhood cancer and other life-threatening diseases, and we’re proud to contribute to their life-saving work.

***

#### **Paint Your Heart Out Palm Beach County**

**Website**: [paintyourheartoutpbc.com](http://www.paintyourheartoutpbc.com/)

We don’t just donate; we volunteer too. Paint Your Heart Out Palm Beach County is a county-wide program that enables individuals, businesses, and organizations to help low-income residents by painting their homes. Over the past 20 years, volunteers and sponsors have painted over 300 homes annually. This initiative strengthens our community, and we’re proud to play a part in it.

***

**Join Us in Making a Difference**

Giving back is more than a commitment; it’s a passion. We invite you to learn more about these incredible organizations and how you can contribute to their missions. Together, we can create a better future for everyone.
