#### **Passionate Developers, Free Resources**

At **Project Sloth**, we’re dedicated to empowering the FiveM community with high-quality, open-source resources. Our focus is on providing free scripts for QBCore and Qbox frameworks, ensuring every developer and server owner has access to tools that enhance their gaming experience. With a thriving community of over **16,000 members** on Discord, Project Sloth is more than just a resource hub—it’s a community-driven movement.

🌐 **Explore Our Work:** [Project Sloth GitHub](https://github.com/projectsloth)\
💬 **Join the Community:** [Project Sloth Discord](https://discord.gg/projectsloth)

Let’s build something amazing together—completely free, completely open, and completely Sloth! 🦥
