# 1OF1 Merch

Get yourself some merch and not only will you be supporting 1OF1 Servers, but also yourself:

* **50%** of your purchase will be **credited to your 1OF1 Servers account**!
  * i.e purchase a [Vintage sweatshirt in our Powered By collection](https://merch.1of1servers.com/en-cad/products/powered-by-sweatshirt-vintage) and have $21.50 prorated back to your 1OF1 Servers account!
* Stylish designs to match all tastes with **3** different **collections**, **6** different **products** in each, and **4 style variations** of each. Take a brief peek at our catalogue!

{% tab title="Collections" %}
- [**1OF1 Collection**](https://merch.1of1servers.com/en-usd/collections/1of1-collection)\
  A fusion of sleek minimalism and bold identity, this collection embodies the essence of 1OF1. Designed with a clean, modern aesthetic, each piece proudly showcases our signature logo or symbol—because being one of a kind never goes out of style.



* [**ON TOP Collection**](https://merch.1of1servers.com/en-usd/collections/on-top-collection)\
  Inspired by you—our incredible community—this collection celebrates the mantra that defines us: _1OF1 ON TOP!_ Bursting with dynamic graphics and vibrant designs crafted from your input, this lineup is more than just apparel—it’s a statement. A tribute to our shared commitment, energy, and the reason why 1OF1 Servers stays on top. Elevate your game, wear the movement!



* [**POWERED BY Collection**](https://merch.1of1servers.com/en-usd/collections/powered-by-collection)\
  Fueling a next-level hosting experience, this collection is all about the energy that makes us truly _1 OF 1_. Every design captures the essence of power, innovation, and the drive to redefine what hosting should be. Whether you’re leveling up your setup or making a statement, this collection is sure to elevate your style!
