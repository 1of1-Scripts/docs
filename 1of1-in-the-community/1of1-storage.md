# 1OF1 Storage

**Discover** 1OF1 Storag&#x65;**: Cloud Storage for Your Gaming Server**

Looking for a simple and secure solution to store and share your game server files? Welcome to 1OF1 Storage, the ultimate cloud storage platform tailored for gaming servers.

#### What is 1OF1 Storage?

1OF1 Storage, created by M**onkeyWhisper**, the owner of [Project Sloth](https://discord.gg/projectsloth) and [1 of 1 Servers](https://discord.gg/1of1servers), was born out of the need for reliable CDN services for FiveM logs, images, videos, and sounds. While built with FiveM in mind, our versatile API can be integrated with almost any platform or game.

#### Why Free?

We believe in giving back to the community that has supported us throughout our journey. 1OF1 Storage, much like [Project Sloth](https://discord.gg/projectsloth), is our way of showing gratitude and contributing to the projects that inspire us.

#### Powered by 1 of 1 Servers

1OF1 Storageoperates on dedicated hardware owned by [1 of 1 Servers](https://discord.gg/1of1servers). Our infrastructure allows us to offer this service free of charge, providing unmatched reliability and performance.
