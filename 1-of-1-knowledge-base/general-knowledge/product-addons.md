{% hint style="success" %}
### [Framework Installation](https://billing.1of1servers.com/cart.php?gid=addons)

\
We will install QBCore framework, QBox, or ESX on your local VPS. If you know how to do this yourself, you may skip this option. \
\
If you want to learn how to do it yourself, QBCore has a guide on their documents found [here.](https://docs.qbcore.org/qbcore-documentation/guides/windows-installation)\
\
Setup takes about 1 hour to complete after we receive your files and we let you know your ticket is being worked on. \
\
<mark style="color:red;">**The server does not come pre-installed with the framework. That is not allowed. We manually log into the dedicated server and install the framework. Package is only available to those who purchase a Dedicated Server and are a new customer, not previously existing (ex. a VPS upgrade to a Dedicated Server, etc).**</mark>
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="success" %}
### [Files Migration](https://1of1servers.com/cart.php?gid=addons)

We will migrate all of your existing files from your old hosting company to your new purchased services. \
\
You can follow [this guide](../../dedicated-servers/dedicated-server-additional-guides/server-transfers-guide-for-fivem-dedicated-servers.md) on how to get started.

<mark style="color:red;">**Package is only available to those who purchase a Dedicated Server and are a new customer, not previously existing (ex. a VPS upgrade to a Dedicated Server, etc).**</mark>
{% endhint %}
