# Login Creation & Linking Discord

Congratulations on your shiny new game server! Now it's time to set it up and connect, and start inviting your friends for the adventure.

## Step 1

### **Create your Login for your Game Server Dashboard**

* You will receive an email after your purchase titled "**Account Created**":
* Click "**Setup Your Account**" and follow the instructions to create your password for the Game Server Dashboard.

## Step 2

### Link your Discord

{% hint style="danger" %}
Go to your [Client Area](https://billing.1of1servers.com/clientarea.php) within the billing portal, and ensure you link your Discord.

This is a critical step to avoid deployment issues and suspension of your newly acquired services!
{% endhint %}

* To link your discord, locate the **Link Social Media** section on the left-hand side of your **Client Area** and click **Link.**
