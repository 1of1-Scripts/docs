## About

The FiveM Project Sloth developers are a group of dedicated and talented individuals who are passionate about creating high-quality scripts <mark style="color:red;">free</mark> for FiveM. \
\
The team is known for their attention to detail and commitment to providing their users with the best possible experience. They are constantly pushing the boundaries of what is possible with FiveM, and their scripts are widely regarded as some of the best available. \
\
Their scripts are regularly updated and supported, and they are always looking for ways to improve and expand their offerings. \
\
PS - MonkeyWhisper owner of 1 of 1 Servers is also an owner of Project Sloth.&#x20;
