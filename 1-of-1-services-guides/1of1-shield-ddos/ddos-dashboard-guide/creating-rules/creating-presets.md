# Creating Presets

## Creating Profiles of Preset Rules

Now that you have learned about creating singular rules and creating a batch of filters, lets step up our game by creating a profile of preset rules.&#x20;

Why create a profile? It's like when you are playing a game, and you lost the boss fight, or got so far without a check point, so you load your previous saved game slot.&#x20;

This is similar, you create different presets of filters so that when a certain attack occurs, you can go back and load your pre-made save that you can deploy instantly to filter the attack.

Once you've configured your ideal rules for your DDoS dashboard, including any custom openings beyond the defaults, we've created presets that allow you to quickly redeploy your settings without having to recreate all your rules.



Click **Manage Presets** and **Create Preset.**&#x20;

Enter a name for the presets and click Ok to save.&#x20;

Now, you can load them by clicking on Load Preset.&#x20;
