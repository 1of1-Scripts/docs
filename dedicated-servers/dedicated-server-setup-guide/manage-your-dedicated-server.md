# Manage Your Dedicated Server

Step 3

Now that you have linked your Discord, it's time to do some basics with your Dedicated Server.

{% hint style="success" %}
Log into your [client area](https://billing.1of1servers.com/clientarea.php?action=products) and click on your Dedicated Server.&#x20;
{% endhint %}

Click on **Manage**, this will take you to a portal where you initially navigate to your Dedicated Server's Dashboard by clicking **Login To Panel**.
