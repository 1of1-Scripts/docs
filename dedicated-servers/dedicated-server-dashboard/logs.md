# Logs

Located on the left-hand side of your screen, below the My Account menu option.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (84).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Logs Menu Location</p></figcaption></figure>

The Logs menu is the same idea as the Login History and your Access Log, it tacks the task completion in detail.

* Date & Time, User, Result, Affects, User IP, Request Source, and Action
