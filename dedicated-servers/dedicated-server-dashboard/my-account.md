# My Account

The My Account Menu will display all your Account Details, Login History, and an Access Log, lets go over what each contain!

* Located on the left-hand side of your screen.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (66).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>My Account Menu Location</p></figcaption></figure>

### Account Details

* Details your acccount information like:
  * Your client name (billing address name), email address linked to your 1Of1 account, Username (Server user ID), Password, Two Factor Authentification, Date & Time Settings, IP Restrictions (if necessary)
    * Here is where you can change your password! Enter your new password and click "**Update**".
  * Notes tool
  * Assigned Servers list (your purchased servers)

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (80).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Account Details Tab Information</p></figcaption></figure>



### Login History

* Catalogue of login attempts, their date & time, IP Address, and User-agent
  * In other words: who did it, when did it happen, where is the person who did it, and what did they do it with.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (81).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Login History</p></figcaption></figure>

### Access Log

* Same idea as your Login History tab, with the addition of:
  * Response Code, Method, Request Source, HTTP Endpoint, & IP
