# Tebex Purchase

## Important – Discord Access Required

Before making your purchase, please make sure you are inside the [**1of1 Scripts Discord server**.](http://discord.gg/1of1scripts)\
Your purchase will automatically assign you the correct role through Tebex.

#### Already Purchased?

No worries.

If you purchased the script but were **not** in the Discord at the time:

1. Join the [1of1 Scripts Discord.](http://discord.gg/1of1scripts)
2. Go to the **#general** channel.
3. Run the command:

```
/claim
```

Once your purchase is verified, you will automatically receive the correct roles.

You must have the **Prometheus** role before you can proceed with access.

***

#### Role Not Assigned Automatically?

If Tebex does not automatically assign your role:

* Open a support ticket in Discord.
* Our team will manually resend the role assignment command for you.

Make sure you receive the proper role before attempting to access any protected resources.
