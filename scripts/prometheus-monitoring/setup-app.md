# Setup App

### Adding Your Server to the App

1. Visit our [App here.](https://app.1of1scripts.com/)
2. Click **Add Server**.
3. Enter:
   * Your **Server Name**
   * Your **Server IP (with port)**

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (26).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

After submitting your server, your **Grafana credentials** will automatically generate and appear in the dashboard under **Grafana Credentials** as below.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-02-25 155825.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

### Accessing Your Metrics Dashboard

1. Go to the [Metrics website.](https://metrics.1of1scripts.com/)
2. Log in using the **Grafana credentials** provided in the app.
3. Once logged in, you will have full access to your Grafana dashboard and metrics.
