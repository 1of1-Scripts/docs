# Home Page Customization

See an example of our [Landing Page](https://connect.monkeywhisper.dev/) to see how each section appears on the live page.

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### Enter Home Page Customization Menu

In the Dashboard, click "**Home Page Customization**"

<figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (92).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Navigation Bar

In this section, you can customize how your navigation bar looks and behaves:

* **Custom Logo**
  * Upload your own logo to replace the default branding.
  * Supported formats: **PNG, JPG, SVG, GIF, WebP**
  * Maximum file size: **10 MB**
  * You’ll see your **current logo** displayed, and you can click it to **remove** or replace it.
* **Store Link Button**
  * Toggle **“Enable Store Link”** to show or hide a **Store** button in the navigation bar.
  * Enter your **Store URL** so that when users click the button, they’re taken directly to your store (Tebex, website, etc.).

Click "**Save**" after you have complete the section!

<figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (98).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
{% endstep %}

### Hero Section

This section controls the main hero area at the top of your page.

* **Enable Hero Image**
  * Turn this on to display a large hero image at the top of the page.
  * You can upload a custom image in **PNG, JPG, SVG, GIF, or WebP** format.
  * Maximum file size: **10 MB**.
*   **Hero Image**

    * Upload the image you want to use as your hero background/visual.

    <figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (99).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
*   **Enable Hero Title**

    * Turn this on to show a custom title inside the hero section.
    * Use the **Hero Title** field (for example: `1of1 Servers RP Page!`) to set the text.

    <figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (100).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
*   **Enable Hero Description**

    * Turn this on to show a short description under the hero title.
    * Use the **Hero Description** field to explain what your server/page is about.

    <figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (101).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Click "**Save**" after you have complete the section\![](http://127.0.0.1:30120/1of1-webqueue/api/health)
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Join Queue & Main Website Buttons

*   **Enable Join Queue Link**

    * Toggle this on to show a **button** that links to your web queue.
    * Label the link/button.

    <figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (104).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

This section lets you highlight your main website with a primary image or video on the page.

*   **Enable Main Website Link**

    * Toggle this on to show a **button** that links to your main website.
    * Set the destination in **Main Website URL** (for example: `https://1of1servers.com/`).
    * Label the link/button.

    <figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (102).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
*   **Enable Main Image**

    * Turn this on to display a main image or video area on the page.
    * You can upload a custom image in **PNG, JPG, SVG, GIF, or WebP** format.
    * Maximum file size: **10 MB**.

    <figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (103).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Click "**Save**" after you have complete the section\![](http://127.0.0.1:30120/1of1-webqueue/api/health)
{% endstep %}

### Page Details/Meta Details

In this section you will give your landing page:

* a Description (for search engine optimization) in 500 characters or less
* a Page Title (title that appears on browser tab) in 200 characters or less
* an icon for the browser tab

Remember to click **Save**!

### Rules Section

This section allows you to add a list of rules that your community may have in place for all members to follow.

Enable the Rules Section to add this to your Landing Page.

Note: We have a default list of general rules that will populate on your landing page once this section is enabled, these can be replaced by adding rules to the section. (See below)

<figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (127).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

If your community has more unique rules, you can add them by:

Click "**Add Rule**" and add it's **Title** and **Description**. Then click "**Add Rule**" to add it to the list.

Drag and drop the rules in the list to organize their priority in descending order. The click "**Save**". You may **Edit** and **Delete** rules using the pencil and bin icons.

### Our Team Section

This section will feature the members of your team, by category (Owner, Staff, Exceptional Supporters)

Enable the Our Team Section to add this to your landing page.

<figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (67).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Add a **Description** introducing your valued team and what they do! Then Save Description.

<figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (68).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Click "**Add Category**" to create a category for specific members, add the **Title** (Owner, Admin, VIP, Staff, etc), and an **Icon**. Click "**Add Category**" then "**Save Categories**" when done.

Drag and drop the categories to organize their display priority (descending order).

<figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (69).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Once your Categories are made, it's time to add the members to their categories respectively.&#x20;

Click "**Add Member**", select their **Category** using the drop-down, add the members **name** and **username**, and their **avatar**. Enable the **Verified Member** for a verified symbol beside their name(s). Click "**Add Member**" to add them to the list.

Drag and drop members to organize their order within their respective categories. Click "**Save Members**". (See result below).

<figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (70).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (71).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
