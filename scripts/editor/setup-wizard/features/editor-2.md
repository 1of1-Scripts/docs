### Ticket System Overview for Staff

On the Landing Page, login and navigate to the "**Support Tickets**" portal using the **Staff Overview** side-bar.

<figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (84).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You can search tickets by:

* key words, or&#x20;
*   filter by:

    * Status (Open, In Progress, Resolved, Closed, All)
    * Priority (Low, Medium, High, Urgent, All)
    * Department (Billing, General, Technical, Support, All)

    <figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (85).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Managing Tickets

Click "**View**" on tickets to enter the tickets thread and manage its contents (status, replies, etc)

<figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (107).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Once inside the tickets overview, you are able to comment (reply), attach files, and see general information about the ticket.

To reply, type in the comment field then click "**Add Reply**" when done.

<figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (108).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The reply will then populate with a timestamp and who made the reply, with the option to modify the reply.

<figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (109).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

To modify the tickets Status, Priority, Department, click "**Edit Ticket**".

<figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (110).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You are able to change the Status, Priority, and Department, as well as add replies. Be sure to save your changes!

<figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (111).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
