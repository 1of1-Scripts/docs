## Dashboard Initial Role Setup

{% hint style="danger" %}
**ATTENTION!**

This is extremely common across almost every Discord server. However, your Discord **must** be configured to automatically assign a specific role to every new member - either when they first join or when they accept your rules/guidelines.

That role will function as the **Dashboard Access Role** below.

Use this role in the following setup, this role will allow users to access the dashboard, to submit tickets or to submit their initial application to earn the whitelist role and be able to join the queue to your server.&#x20;

This role has NO priority!
{% endhint %}

Take note of the role that is assigned to every member in your Discord when they first join your Discord server, or the role assigned when they agree to rules/guidelines to engage in your server.&#x20;

Our example of this role will be "Verified", yours could be the same or be "Member", etc.&#x20;

All the roles in your Discord will populate in the "**Add Roles**" selection menu on the Web Queue Dashboard.

Enter the **Dashboard Roles Setup** menu.

<figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (252).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Click "**Add Roles**" to add the base role (applied to everyone upon joining your Discord, our example will use "Verified")

<figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (253).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Select the role that is assigned to every member, then click "**Add Selected**" and Save your configuration.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (255).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
