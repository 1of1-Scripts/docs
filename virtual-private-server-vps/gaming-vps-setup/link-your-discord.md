# Link Your Discord

## Step 2

{% hint style="danger" %}
Please ensure that you link your Discord account before proceeding. You can do this via your Customer Dashboard, accessible [here](https://billing.1of1servers.com/clientarea.php).

This is a critical step to avoid deployment issues and suspension of your newly acquired services!
{% endhint %}

To link your discord, locate the **Link Social Media** section on the left-hand side of your Client Area and click **Link**

## Backup your files daily

It is crucial to regularly back up your data, including database files such as Heidi or SQL. You can find our guide on backing up your database [here](../../1-of-1-game-server-guides/fivem/sql-backup.md). We recommend using backup services like GitHub or Google Drive.

Please be aware that your data is not automatically safeguarded, so relying solely on our VPS backups is not advisable. System failures can occur, and we do not want you to risk losing your important data.
{% endhint %}
