# After Purchase

## Step 1

After placing your order, let's [Continue To your Client Area](https://billing.1of1servers.com/login) to begin the setup process!

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Order Placed.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Initial Purchase Confirmation</p></figcaption></figure>

Once you have purchased a service from us, the [My Dashboard](https://billing.1of1servers.com/index.php?rp=/login) will be your one-stop portal for everything to do with your 1 of 1 Servers experience.&#x20;

Depending on your active services, this dashboard is a primary gateway to access your other dashboards, it is also where you will modify the settings of your 1Of1 Servers account. It displays:&#x20;

* All your servers; those you own and those shared with others.
* Account information like;
  * Billing info such as invoices, billing address, and payment info;
  * Account name, linked media accounts, support tickets;
  * Domain registration, contacts, and recent news.
* Portals to dashboards that manage your purchased 1 of 1 services.

Each of your services will have its very own dashboard to uniquely tailor your settings to that server's needs.&#x20;
