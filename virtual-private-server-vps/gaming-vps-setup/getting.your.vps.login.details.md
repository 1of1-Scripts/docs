# Getting Your VPS Login Details

## Step 5

{% hint style="danger" %}
Please wait 3-5 minutes after your installation is complete!

**Check your junk/spam/promotions/inbox folders for the incoming email.**
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="info" %}
To find your VPS log information you will need to go to your email inbox and, as previously mentioned, locate the email you will have received after your installation has finished titled "Your VPS Server is Ready at 1of1 Servers!".&#x20;
{% endhint %}

**It will contain:**

* Your IPv4 Address (4 - 12 digits): ###.###.###.###
* Port (4 digits): ####
* Username:&#x20;
  * "Administrator" (Windows) **or** "Root" (Debian/Ubuntu)
* Password (20 characters): 9HL0n2Wjm6n2JA1dTr4a (example)

{% hint style="info" %}
If you still have not received the email, that's totally fine, you can use your dashboard to grab all the information you need to log into [Remote Desktop.](../../dedicated-servers/dedicated-server-setup-guide/remote-desktop-connection-rdp-login/remote-desktop-connection.md)\
\
Your IP can be found on your dashboard as Primary IPv4. Go to your dashboard homepage and click "Network" (see below).\
\
Use that for [Remote Desktop](../../dedicated-servers/dedicated-server-setup-guide/remote-desktop-connection-rdp-login/remote-desktop-connection.md) along with the default username Administrator. \
\
For your password, you'll need to reset it. Learn how to to that [here.](../vps-dashboard/password-reset.md)
{% endhint %}
